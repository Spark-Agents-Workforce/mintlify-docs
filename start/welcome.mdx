## What we are and aren't

Worth two minutes, because this place runs on different physics than the tools you're used to renting.

**WE ARE** the team that did all four jobs: ran the subscription programs for the biggest CPG subscription brands on Shopify, built their stores, built the cart software 100+ brands run on, and then built the platform itself — and ran it, with real brands billing real customers through it. The full story, with the receipts, is on [the About page](https://opensubs.com/about). The short version: we've been inside every layer of this machine, which is why we can hand it to you with a straight face.

**WE ARE NOT** a subscription platform. Recharge, Skio, Stay, Loop — those are platforms: they run the machine and charge you a percentage to stand near it. We hand you the machine. Once it's yours, your bill doesn't know your revenue exists.

**WHAT WE TAKE:** the membership. Flat, annual — never a percentage, never per-transaction.

**WHAT WE NEVER TOUCH:** your revenue, your data, your infrastructure, your customers. Not "we promise not to" — we architecturally can't. It deploys to your accounts. We couldn't reach into it if we wanted to.
