## What this page gives you

- What a membership is mechanically: a digital product + a selling plan + a subscription contract. No box, no fulfillment — and your engine already bills it at 0%.
- Set it up: create the membership product, configure the plan, price and frequency; what appears in your admin and the subscriber portal.
- The entitlement layer: billing events drive member status — paid means active, failed means suspended, cancelled means access expires at period end. The honest split between what the platform does and what Shopify-side wiring you own (with the recipe).
- Dunning for memberships: a failed card is a suspended member, so grace periods matter more here than anywhere.
- The contrast: the incumbents bill memberships for a percentage — and hand you a DIY workflow guide for the actual membership part. Here the whole loop runs on infrastructure you own.
