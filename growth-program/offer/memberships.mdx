## What this lesson gives you

- The model: a recurring fee that unlocks perks — member pricing, early access, free shipping, exclusive drops. Costco/Prime logic at DTC scale.
- The economics: the membership fee is high-margin recurring revenue AND a frequency lever — members buy more often to "use" their fee. Sunk-cost loyalty, priced.
- Offer design: what to charge, what to gate, annual vs monthly, and the member-shipping play vs the free-shipping-threshold trap.
- When NOT to: low purchase frequency, thin catalog, perks that leak more margin than they retain — the cost-per-retained-month test decides.
- The mechanics handoff: your platform already bills memberships as subscription contracts; the run-it page has the setup.
