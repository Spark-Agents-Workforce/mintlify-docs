You made it here one of two ways. Either you deployed the platform and your subscription revenue now runs at 0% — or you skipped straight to the part where you grow it. Both doors work. That's the point of this whole property.

One more thing, said out loud: the journey you just walked — the script that cut your fee, the look inside the machine, the payoff in your own logs — WAS a retention flow. Welcome email, value delivery, habit formation. It worked on you. Now you get to build the same machine for your subscribers.
